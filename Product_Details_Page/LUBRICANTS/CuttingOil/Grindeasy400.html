MatrolÂ® Grindeasy 400 is a water soluble mineral cutting oil fortified with appropriate additives to prevent bacterial degradation of the emulsion. It also prevents rusting of the work piece and machine tool beds. It is suitable for mid to heavy duty cutting of major ferrous and non-ferrous metals .It forms a stable emulsion in both normal and hard water conditions with compatibility with water up to 400 ppm. For general machining operations the emulsion is normally prepared with 5% oil and for grinding operation the emulsion has 2% of oil.
