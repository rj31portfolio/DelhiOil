Hydraulic Oil 32

Hydraulic Oil 32 is a ultra-high performance hydraulic fluid formulated with severely hydro treated base stocks. The unique zinc free and ash free anti wear additive system provides enhanced protection for components as compared to traditional hydraulic oil meeting DIN 51524 Part III (HVLP) and Parker Denison HF-0.

Hydraulic Oil 46

Hydraulic Oil 46 PREMIUM is blended from deep hydrofinished base stocks and select additives. The additives are designed for optimum compressor operation. The additives are used to enhance the oxidation stability, anti-rust characteristics, anti-wear properties of the oil. The oil has natural demulsification property, low foaming tendency and quick air release property.

Hydraulic Oil 68

Hydraulic Oil 68 is blended from deep hydrofinished base stocks and select additives. The additives are designed for optimum compressor operation. The additives are used to enhance the oxidation stability, anti-rust characteristics, anti-wear properties of the oil. The oil has natural demulsification property, low foaming tendency and quick air release property

PP Primer

Polypropylene adhesive primer is designed for use on difficult-to-bond low surface energy plastics such as polypropylene, polyethylene. Reduces surface tension for a better bond Promotes adhesion to Polypropylene & Polyethylene

Quenching Oil

These quenching oils are blends of refined base oils and contain no additives. MATROL quenching oil is recommended for general purpose quenching of components and is particularly suitable for quenching of cyandied parts. MATROL Quenching oil is suitable in situations when lower quenching speed is desired.

Rubber Processing Oil

RUBBER PROCESS OIL used by manufacturer of finished rubber product, to aid processing and RUBBER EXTENDERS OIL used by raw material manufacturers to soften polymers.

Sewing Machine Oil

Sewing machine oil is mostly used for lubricating various parts of a sewing machine especially when the machine gets jammed. Processed using qualitative raw materials, our sewing machine oils are used for improving the performance and effectiveness of operations of a sewing machine.p>

Transformer Oil

Transformer Oil is a highly refined, naphthenic transformer oil for use where insulating oils meeting the IEC 60296:2003 transformer oil specification are recommended. Transformer Oil does not contain polychlorinated biphenyls (PCBs).

EDM Oil

Electric Discharge Machining Oil is a metal removal process involving the removal of material from the work piece by a series of controlled electric discharges or sparks. The tool and the work piece are insulated by a special dielectric fluid. These fluid are formulated with a highly refined, narrow cut paraffinic base stock with excellent oxidation stability which helps reduce the formation of oxidation products that will alter the dielectric strength of the fluid.
